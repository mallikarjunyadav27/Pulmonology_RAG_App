What is RLHF Model Training?

The RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) model learns from SME ratings to predict answer quality. It uses SBERT embeddings and Logistic Regression to score and re-rank candidate answers.

Training Process: The model analyzes rated interactions, learns patterns, and improves response selection accuracy.
